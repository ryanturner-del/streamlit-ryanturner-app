## App Description 
The app that I will be making will be a expense/budgeting app that will help students with everyday expenses

## Intended Users
The intended users of this app will be any student or individual that is looking for a smarter way to budget their monthly expenses like grociers, gas, and subscirptions

## Planned Features

1 Categorize spending into catagories such as food, gas, rent, etc
2 Display total budget and how much money is left over for savings account, brokerage account
3 Be able to put info into various charts that visualize the money and how it is being allocated
